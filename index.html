🛡️ Game Rules

⚔️ 1. Griefing
"to grief" — to ruin other players' experiences
Minor Griefing
  • Building on top of everyone's builds
  • Placing spikes around the spawn area
✓ No action (can be prevented by server owners)
Standard Griefing
  • Builds that kill all players on spawn (teleport traps, microwaves)
⛔ 14-day ban
Major Griefing
  • Anything that makes the server unplayable (crashers, laggers)
🚫 Permanent ban
🔞 2. NSFW Content
Minor
  • Slightly suggestive builds, ambiguous shapes
⚠️ 3-day ban
Major
  • Detailed NSFW builds or audios
  • Hosting servers dedicated to NSFW content
🚫 Permanent ban
💻 3. Exploiting
Minor
  • Third-party tools with little to no advantage
✓ No action
Major
  • Injecting code to grief servers (e.g. flinging everyone)
🚫 Permanent ban
🐛 4. Abusing Glitches
  • E.g. using a glitch to kick/ban moderators from your server
⛔ 7-day ban → Permanent on 2nd offense
📢 5. Degrading the Game's Reputation
  • Spreading misinformation or destructive criticism that drives players away
🚫 Permanent ban
📋 6. Roblox TOS

Follow Roblox Terms of Service at all times while playing.
